| **2. Finding Discontinuity Points**              | **Find all points of discontinuity of $f$, where $f(x) = \begin{cases} 2x + 3 & \text{if } x \le 2 \ 2x - 3 & \text{if } x > 2 \end{cases}$**                                                                        |                 |
