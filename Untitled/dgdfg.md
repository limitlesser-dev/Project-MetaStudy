#Q #AOSR/7hct3

? #AOSR/7hct3/m/58k3

$$ \frac{1}{40} = \frac{WB \times 1000}{15000} $$
$$ WB = \frac{1 \times 15000}{40 \times 1000} = \frac{15}{40} = \frac{3}{8} = 0.375 \text{ gm} $$
